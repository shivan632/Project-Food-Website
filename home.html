Our special vegetarian Chowmein is made with fresh veggies, tender noodles, and bold flavors!

Pasta Alfredo

Indulge in the rich, creamy delight of our vegetarian Pasta Alfredo with Parmesan cheese and butter.

Made with fresh cream, garlic, and a touch of nutmeg, this classic Italian dish is a comfort food favorite. Add sautéed mushrooms or spinach for an extra burst of flavor.

Mango Smoothie

Blended with ripe mangoes, yogurt, and a touch of honey, this creamy smoothie is a tropical burst of freshness in every sip.

Rich in vitamin C and fiber, it boosts immunity and digestion. For extra creaminess, add a splash of coconut milk or a scoop of vanilla protein powder!

Green Tea

Steeped from antioxidant-rich leaves, green tea offers a refreshing, earthy flavor with metabolism-boosting benefits.

Contains L-theanine for calm focus and catechins for heart health. For best flavor, steep at 175°F (80°C) for 2-3 minutes to avoid bitterness.

Ice Cream Sundae

Indulge in a classic ice cream sundae—cool, creamy scoops topped with syrup, whipped cream, and a cherry for the perfect sweet treat.

Fun fact: The sundae was invented in the late 1800s as a Sunday treat when soda fountains couldn't sell ice cream sodas on the Sabbath. For a lighter version, try frozen yogurt with fresh fruit and dark chocolate shavings!
